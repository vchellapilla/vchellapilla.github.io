Hi! My name is Varshini Chellapilla. I am a journalist, writer and lifelong idealist.

I am a senior majoring in print & digital journalism with a minor in English at the Pennsylvania State University.

I have been writing stories since I was six-years-old, with a tale about a cat stealing a mango being my earliest masterpiece.

As a journalist, I am interested in the bigger picture and the details that pull it together. I like finding answers for people that will help them make important decisions about their lives and societies.

I have writen stories about the increasing air pollution in Pittsburgh, the state of homeless college students at Penn State, the Amish way of life for disabled members of their community, and the science behind rewriting DNA at a researcher's lab.
