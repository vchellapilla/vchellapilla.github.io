Hi! My name is Varshini. I am a writer and journalist.

I have been writing stories since I was six-years-old, with a tale about a cat stealing a mango being my earliest masterpiece.

I am a recent graduate with a B.A. in Print & Digital Journalism and a minor in English from the Pennsylvania State University. I have worked in communications, publishing and journalism.

My work has been published by the Penn Memory Center, Penn Medicine, the Centre Daily Times, Penn State News, The Daily Collegian and PublicSource.
